Puspalal Smriti Park


Puspalal Park, one of the pleasant site to visit, lies at centraql part of Hetauda. ONe can enjoy the park by observing beautiful flowers and herbal plants in peace, healthy and family environment. Surrounded by Ashok trees, the park has a pond at the centre. The park is established in memory of late Puspalal Shrestha, founder of Nepal Communist Party. Inside the park there is Puspalal Memorial Library.
