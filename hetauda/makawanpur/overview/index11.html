Introduction


Hetauda Municipality is stepping ahead with development of basic infrastructure and physical development of basic infrastructure and physical development being connected with two major highways. Mahendra and Tribhuwan highways with historical and religious background of goddess mala Bhutandevi and Hedamba out of which the name Hetauda originated, This city has been developing as a regional headquarter of Central Development Region in recent days. The formal development of this city started after it was declared as 'Nagar Panchayat' in 2026 BS and District Headquarter in 2039 BS. As this city with its physical racial and linguistic diversity lies in the ventral part of our country, it is being developed as modern city.
