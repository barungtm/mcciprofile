Introduction to MCCI



Nepal, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal, is a landlocked sovereign state located in South Asia. With an area of 147,181 square kilometers and a population of approximately 27 million, Nepal is the world's 93rd largest country by land mass and the 41st most populous country. It is located in the Himalayas and bordered to the north by the People's Republic of China, and to the south, east, and west by the Republic of India. Specifically, the Indian states of Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, West Bengal, and Sikkim border Nepal, while across the Himalayas lies the Tibetan Autonomous Region. Kathmandu is the nation's capital and largest metropolis. Nepal is known as Himalayan country. Nepal is divided in 14 Zone including 75 District, which is divided into five Development Regions. The beautiful Himalayan ranges the highest mountain of the world "Mount Everest" (8,848 mt.) is in Nepal. Among five Development Regions, Makwanpur District lies in Mid Development Region.  
For the overall Development and promotion of trade and Industry of the region, Makawanpur Chamber of Commerce & Industry  (MCC) came into existence in 1966 (2023 B.S) with the name of Hetauda Byapar Sangh. With the processing of time and increase of scope, the name of chamber was changed into Makawanpur Chamber of Commerce & Industry (MCCI).

MCCI, a representative organization of all private sector business community of the region is a non-political, non-governmental, non-profit making service oriented organization for the promotion of trade and industry. So, MCCI is committed to the promotion of commerce and Industry of the region in particular and overall economic development of the country in general. MCCI has been making significant contribution to the promotion of trade and Industry. It has been active in the field of social services also. As a result of this, it has been receiving help and assistance from all the concerned of the district. MCCI is an actively affiliated member of Federation of Nepalese Commerce & Industry (FNCCI).
