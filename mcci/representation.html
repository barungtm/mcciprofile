MCCI Info

Representation


MCCI is now in a key position to work for the overall development of the trade and Industry in the zone. MCCI has representation in different local, regional administration, organization, educational and health institutions for the promotion of overall social development including trade & Industry.
Community Development Activities
Being a part of society, MCCI has made a significant contribution for the development of education, culture and health facility. It has made financial contributions for the physical development of the Makwanpur Multiple Campus. Similarly it has also contributed financially to make a building for Hetauda Hospital and its regular income and for blood bank building. It has been working for installing CCTV in major trading places of the Hetauda City too.
MCCI frequently helps by extending financial & other contributions to local clubs to conduct regional, national and international level programs
