Promotional Services


MCCI has been providing the following services to its member entrepreneurs and general public.

  • Trainings, Workshop and Seminars:
    The chamber has been conducting continuously different trainings, workshops and seminars for its members and for non-member entrepreneurs. This type of trainings and workshops are organized in the partnership with different agencies and partner organizations such as FNCCI, GIZ, PPPEU, IEM, AEC, HAM, ILO, SNV, TPC and local governmental & non-governmental organization. MCCI always welcomes new projects/organizations for partnership.
  • Training Facilities:
    MCCI frequently organizes Industrial Management Training to strengthen industry to improve in quality production process, human resource development, productivity and so on. MCCI organizes these training programs at work place or out of work place.
  • Exhibition:
    MCCI organizes industrial trade fair & exhibitions for the promotion of market identification at local and regional level of the benefit its member and non-member entrepreneurs. MCCI is planning to organize exhibitions on regular basis.
  • Information and Documentation Service:
    MCCI has a separate unit for information and documentation services, which provides business information as per of the members.
  • Conference Facilities:
    MCCI has well equipped three conference halls with sound system. Today MCCI has become an important venue for meetings and conferences. So many other organizations of the district also make use of the conference hall as and when required.
  • Publications:
    MCCI publishes monthly bulletins as well as member Directory, Business Directory, Souvenir and so on.
  • Secretariat :

MCCI has permanent secretariat consisting eleven fulltime employees and two part time. It also hires employees on contract basis. The secretariat is headed by an Executive Secretary. The overall secretariat is fun under the guidance and supervision of the Secretary General.
