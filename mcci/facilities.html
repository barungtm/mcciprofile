Facilities
MCCI, established as an umbrella organization of all industries and trade person of the district to safeguard and promote their rights and duties, has been helping it’s member as far as possible with its available resources by providing following business Services.

  • Free legal advices.
  • Free of charge tax consultation services.
  • One Stop Shop.
  • National/International educational tours.
  • Identification and solution of real problems of Women entrepreneurs or trade personnel's. 
  • Email & Internet.
  • Computer & Fax.
  • National/International Business information.
  • Publication of Business directory/bulletin.
  • Life insurance Service
  • Liaison/dispute handing services.
  • Round table meeting.
  • Dispute handing services between Management and Labor unions.
  • Many more activities for women entrepreneurs.
